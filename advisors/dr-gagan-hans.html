Research and Academic Contributions

Dr. Gagan Hans specializes in cutting-edge research in neuroimaging in psychiatry, metabolic abnormalities in depression, and Magnetic Resonance Spectroscopy studies. His work bridges the gap between neuroscience and clinical psychiatry, contributing to a deeper understanding of mental health conditions.

His research focuses on psycho-radiology and the application of neuroimaging techniques in understanding psychiatric disorders. He has made significant contributions to the field of genetics and genomics in psychiatry, exploring the biological underpinnings of mental health conditions.

Community and Professional Contributions

Dr. Hans is actively involved in public mental health education through public lectures, organizing conferences and CME programs, student wellness initiatives, and media contributions on mental health awareness. His commitment extends beyond clinical practice to fostering a broader understanding of mental health in society.

He has been instrumental in community psychiatry initiatives and has contributed to various mental health awareness programs. His work in the field of common mental disorders has helped bridge the gap between specialized psychiatric care and community-based mental health services.

Editorial and Academic Roles

As an Invited Co-Editor for the Indian Journal of Social Psychiatry, Dr. Hans contributes to the advancement of psychiatric knowledge and research dissemination in the Indian context. His editorial work focuses on promoting research in social psychiatry and community mental health.

Current Research Projects

Dr. Hans is currently serving as Principal Investigator for an ICMR Project Grant (2023-2026), which demonstrates his continued commitment to advancing psychiatric research and evidence-based practice in India.
