Academic and Research Contributions

Dr. Biswa Ranjan Mishra is a renowned expert in geriatric psychiatry and cognitive neuroscience with over three decades of clinical and research experience. He has been at the forefront of research in dementia, Alzheimer's disease, and age-related cognitive disorders.

His work focuses on understanding the neurobiological mechanisms underlying cognitive decline in aging and developing innovative assessment and intervention strategies for elderly patients with psychiatric disorders. He has pioneered research in the Indian context on cultural factors affecting mental health in the elderly population.

Current Research Projects

Dr. Mishra is currently leading several research initiatives focused on early detection of cognitive impairment, development of culturally appropriate cognitive assessment tools for Indian elderly, and investigation of neuroprotective strategies in neurodegenerative diseases.

His recent work involves collaborative research on biomarker identification for Alzheimer's disease and the development of digital health interventions for cognitive enhancement in aging populations.
