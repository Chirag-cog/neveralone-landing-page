Professional Experience

  • Previously worked at Safdarjung Hospital
  • Over 20 years of experience in healthcare and medical education

Key Achievements

  • Set up Pharmacology Labs and museum
  • Organized numerous workshops on pharmacovigilance, clinical research, and teaching methodology
  • Published multiple papers in peer-reviewed medical journals
  • Developed MedBeat HealthConnect platform using AI for clinical decision support

Current Role & Vision

She has developed MedBeat HealthConnect, a healthcare platform using artificial intelligence for clinical decision support. The platform aims to capture comprehensive medical histories more thoroughly than traditional medical consultations.

Her professional focus is on leveraging technology to improve medical care and education, with a particular emphasis on using AI to enhance patient information gathering and clinical insights.
