We're Here Before You Ask for Help

Most people wait until they're overwhelmed to reach out. By then, it can feel impossible. We believed there had to be a better way, so we created it.

Never Alone is a lifeline in your pocket, starting with a chat on WhatsApp, guided by smart AI built by AIIMS Delhi, and continuing with real psychologists and psychiatrists who listen without judgment.

It started with a simple idea: mental wellness should be accessible, immediate, and human-centered. Today, we combine technology, expert care, and community partnerships to make that vision a reality.
