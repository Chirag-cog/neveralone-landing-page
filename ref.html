Our Foundation

Global Centre for Integrative Health has been formed with Collaborative Vision of All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi; The Chopra Foundation, USA and Mental Health Foundation (India). In this synergistic relationship, these apex institutions intent to unite for Research, Education and Wellness Activities, with a particular focus on Mental Health and Well-Being of AIIMS students in addition to Other Educational Institutions. As an outcome of the MoU signed on 10th July, 2023, the Parties have agreed to implement a Mental Health and Wellness Service titled caring connections "Never Alone" which will provide Al-driven, Confidential, Accessible Mental Health Consultations and Wellness Services for students using a digital platform via WhatsApp. The Chopra Foundation will support the initiative by providing wellness content for the service and MHF (India) shall implement "Never Alone" mental health service and AIIMS Delhi will Provide logistical support as necessary to facilitate the integration of the service.
